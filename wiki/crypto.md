**Domain hub: the crypto market — its foundations, its history of manias
and collapses, its sectors, its security battlefield, and its
institutional future. Start here; every crypto page in the vault is one
hop away.**

The one-paragraph map: [[bitcoin]] created trustless digital money
([[blockchain]] + [[proof-of-work]]); [[ethereum]] generalized it into
programmable finance ([[smart-contracts]]); everything since is the market
repeatedly discovering what that enables — and what it breaks — in
four-year waves of mania, fraud, collapse, and consolidation
([[crypto-market-cycles]]), arriving by 2024-26 at the institutional era:
ETFs, regulated [[stablecoins]], and Wall Street tokenizing its own assets.
