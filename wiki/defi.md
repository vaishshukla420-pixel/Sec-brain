**DeFi rebuilds financial services — exchange, lending, derivatives, asset
issuance — as permissionless smart contracts with no institution in the
middle: auditable by anyone, usable by anyone, exploitable by anyone.**

The core primitives, all born or hardened in [[defi-summer]]:

- **DEXs / AMMs** (Uniswap model): passive liquidity pools replace market
  makers; anyone can list or LP.
- **Lending markets** (Aave, Compound): overcollateralized borrowing with
  algorithmic rates and on-chain liquidations.
- **Liquid staking** (Lido): stake [[proof-of-stake]] assets, receive a
  usable receipt token — the largest TVL category.
- **Perpetuals DEXs** (Hyperliquid's rise this cycle): the CeFi casino,
  on-chain.
- **Stablecoins** ([[stablecoins]]): DeFi's base money and unit of account.

Strengths proven by fire: transparent reserves and open liquidation logic
meant DeFi processed the 2022 deleveraging without bailouts while opaque
CeFi lenders died ([[ftx-collapse]]). Standing weaknesses: contract and
oracle risk ([[smart-contract-security]]), governance capture, MEV
extraction, and a persistent pattern where "decentralized" protocols have
quite centralized admin keys and front-ends — the gap regulators now probe
([[crypto-regulation]]). Composability remains both superpower and systemic
risk: money legos stack, and so do their failure modes.
